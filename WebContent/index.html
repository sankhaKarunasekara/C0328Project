FaceIt is a game specially designed
for Ordinary Level students.


It will provide multiple choice questions that are related to Ordinary level examination. Students who are playing this game are supposed to answer those questions within a
specific time.

Questions will cover the Major subject matters of Science, History, Languages, Mathematics and etc.

Answers provided by Students will be assessed. Depending on the marks gained by students their progress will be graded.

To make this game more interesting there will be Special Awards, Bonus Questions, Additional Helps and Figures for the students who perform well in the game.
